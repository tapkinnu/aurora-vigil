# Aurora Vigil — Master Brief

Created: 2026-06-16

## High concept

A 3D superhero sandbox about a solar-charged guardian, **The Lumen**, learning to protect Meridian City through flight, rescue, disaster response, and escalating story missions. The fantasy is inspired by the broad archetype of a high-powered flying protector, but every name, costume element, symbol, city, faction, and narrative beat must be original.

## Pillars

1. **Joy of flight:** fast, controllable, readable city traversal with vertical objectives.
2. **Heroism as systems:** dynamic rescues, disasters, drone threats, and public trust consequences.
3. **Progressive power curve:** start with flight/boost; unlock radiant beam, sonic burst, aegis field, rescue lift, orbit sprint, and late-game citywide interventions.
4. **Original city mythology:** Meridian City, aurora-energy infrastructure, civic AI grid, orbital weather anomalies, and anti-hero factions unique to this IP.

## Minimum vertical slice

- One playable city district.
- Free flight, boost, ascend/descend, camera follow.
- Three dynamic events.
- Two story missions with objectives and XP rewards.
- Level-up/unlock loop with at least three powers.
- HUD for mission, powers, level, city events.
- Screenshot capture + validation harness.

## Full release target

- Multi-district city sandbox.
- 8+ dynamic event archetypes.
- 6+ story missions with recurring original cast.
- Real 3D hero/civilian/enemy models, no 2D cards as world actors.
- Generated/provenance-tracked audio and music.
- Save/load for progression, missions, unlocked powers, and city trust.
