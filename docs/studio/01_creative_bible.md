# Aurora Vigil — Creative Bible

## Working title
Aurora Vigil

## Original hero
**The Lumen** — a solar-aurora empowered guardian wearing teal-black armor with gold ion mantle fins. No caped red-blue iconography, no chest letters, no Krypton/alien-origin copy, no Superman names, symbols, villains, or lore.

## Setting
**Meridian City**, a near-future vertical coastal city powered by aurora collectors and civic drone infrastructure. The skyline is bright teal glass, warm gold transit lines, and storm-dark ocean haze.

## Factions
- **Civic Grid:** city infrastructure AI, usually helpful but vulnerable to corruption.
- **The Null Choir:** anti-augmentation extremists using acoustic disruption tech.
- **Heliostat Syndicate:** corporate energy smugglers exploiting aurora collectors.
- **Storm Children:** civilians changed by an atmospheric event, not villains by default.

## Power identity
- Flight: ion-lift aura and gold contrails.
- Radiant Beam: concentrated aurora light, not eye lasers in superhero-IP styling.
- Sonic Burst: pressure-ring clap for crowd control and debris clearing.
- Aegis Field: teal-gold rescue shield around civilians/structures.
- Rescue Lift: non-lethal carry/stabilize ability.
- Orbit Sprint: late-game hypersonic traversal with careful collateral limits.

## Tone
Heroic, hopeful, kinetic, and civic-minded. The core fantasy is protecting people under pressure, not unchecked destruction.
