# Aurora Vigil — Radio & Civilian Bark Lines

**Version:** 1.0  
**Created:** 2026-06-16  
**Status:** Draft — game-creative kanban output

---

## Purpose

These bark lines are short audio-voice or text-flavor snippets that play during gameplay to enhance immersion, communicate city mood, and reflect the consequences of The Lumen's actions. They fall into two categories:

1. **Radio chatter** — Emergency dispatch, Grid communications, faction broadcasts (heard through The Lumen's emergency channel)
2. **Civilian barks** — Overheard dialogue from NPCs in the world (played during flight proximity or rescue events)

Lines are tagged by context, trust level, and faction relevance.

## Section 2: Civilian Barks (World NPCs)

### 2A. Positive / Gratitude (Heard during flight or near rescue sites)

> **"Look! Up there — that's them. That's The Lumen. My kid drew a picture of 'em yesterday. Didn't think I'd see for real."**  
> — Conduit Row shopkeeper, midday

> **"I was on that platform when it gave way. I still don't understand how they caught me — I was falling, and then I just... wasn't. My hands are still shaking."**  
> — Foundry Quarter worker, post-rescue

> **"The Lumen flew right through the aurora to get to us. Right through it. They didn't even hesitate."**  
> — Shelf resident, after Aurora Storm

> — Shelf resident, evening

## Implementation Notes

- **Radio lines** play through The Lumen's emergency channel audio — slightly filtered, with Grid frequency artifacts. They should feel like they're coming through a device, not narrated.
- **Civilian barks** are spatial audio — heard when The Lumen flies within ~50 meters of the NPC. Volume and reverb scale with distance and environment (canyon echo in the Shelf, open-air in the Drift).
- **Trust gating:** The bark system checks city trust level before selecting from the pool. High-trust barks are more common at high trust, but low-trust barks can still appear (not everyone agrees).
- **Faction gating:** Some barks only appear if the player's reputation with the relevant faction is above a threshold. Storm Children barks require at least neutral Storm Children reputation.
- **Cooldown:** No bark line repeats within 10 minutes of gameplay. The pool is large enough to avoid repetition in normal play sessions.
- **Voice casting:** Each bark category should use distinct voice actors. Radio dispatch uses a consistent voice (the player's Grid liaison). Civilian barks use a rotating cast reflecting Meridian's diversity — age, accent, gender, and emotional register should vary widely.
